Hnefatafl 11 x 11
Though mentioned in several Norse sagas, the rules for this Scandenavian tafl game were never explicitly recorded. This is just one of many reconstructed variants.
Tablut 7 x 7
The best documented tafl game, Tablut was played well into the 18th century and its rules served as the basis for later tafl reconstructions. Originating in Sweden, the pieces were referred to as "Swedes and Russains".

Base Rules

  • All pieces may move as far as possible horizontally and vertically. Attacker goes first.
  • Capture by trapping opponent horizontally or vertically. Opponent is not captured if they move into the trap.
  • Attacker wins by capturing the defender King.
  • Defender wins when the King escapes to an edge tile.
  • Defender wins when the King escapes to a corner tile
